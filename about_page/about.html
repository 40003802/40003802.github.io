About Us

We are Bean and Brew, a small coffee store chain with multiple stores across Yorkshire and Northumberland.

Our company began in the late 1970s as a single store based in Harrogate, Yorkshire, and we were one of the first companies to use fair-trade coffee and all organic milk for our products. Since then, we have expanded our business to multiple stores, and have continued to deliver our services to the highest standard possible. See our services in the section below.

Coffee Shops - Our stores are our most important aspect. Without them, we would not be Bean and Brew!

Small Restaurants - A more recent addition to our business are our Restaurants found in Leeds.

Baking Lessons - This year, we have started providing baking lessons. Click here to find out more!

Takeaway and Booking services - Through this site, you can book takeaways, and also book spaces in our stores!
